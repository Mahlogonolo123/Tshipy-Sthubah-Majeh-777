    My Vision

    Saying Goodbye To Feeling Powerless

    It is a very human trait to feel sorry for yourself to feel powerless in the face of forces outside of our control

    It may be your circumstances

    It may be society and social structures

    It may be fate

    It may be specific people

    Life is often hard

    Life is not necessary fair

    But as long as you see yourself as a helpless casualty of circumstance, you giving away all you power

    Whatever has happened,has happened

    You have no control over past events, Do I?

    Have control over your response

    You are the one in charge

    You have the power

    Your decisions and actions are yours to celebrate or sometimes to regret and learn fron

    My Mission

    To have the necessary skills and competencies to successfully learn, deal with life and to become a well-adjusted citizen, who contributes successfully and meaningfully to society and my community

    I have the purpose on earth, therefore i should strive to find out what my purpose is. I must fill certain positions in life and in communities, therefore i need to develop myself to fill these positions

    I am gradually growing and improving my personalities, skills and knowledge of myself, life and work, while I am increasing my knowledge and improving myself, I became more valuable in life and in my worksituation.
